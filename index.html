Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2025

PomoSense is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection and Use

PomoSense is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage patterns or browsing history
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Any data that can identify you personally

Data Storage

All app settings, preferences, and timer configurations are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not synced to any cloud service or server.

App Lock Feature

PomoSense includes an App Lock feature that uses Apple's Screen Time API to help you stay focused by blocking selected apps during work sessions.

Important Privacy Notes:

  • We do not collect or access any information about which apps you choose to block
  • Your app blocking preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Apple's Screen Time API operates entirely on your device
  • We cannot see or access your Screen Time data

Third-Party Services

PomoSense uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Anonymous crash reporting and basic analytics
  • Data Collected: Anonymous crash logs and app performance data
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

StoreKit (Apple)

  • Purpose: Processing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Collected: Purchase information handled securely by Apple
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

PomoSense does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Our app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Advertising

PomoSense does not display any advertisements. We generate revenue solely through optional premium subscriptions.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal data to secure or breach. All your settings remain on your device and are protected by your device's own security measures.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data for you to request, modify, or delete from our servers. You can always delete the app to remove all local data from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
